How to Add Your Signature to Gmail

  1. Copy your signature using the “Copy Signature” button above
  2. Open Gmail in your web browser
  3. Click the gear icon (⚙️) in the top right corner
  4. Select “See all settings”
  5. Scroll down to the “Signature” section
  6. Click “Create new” to add a signature
  7. Give it a name (e.g. “Sunflower Signature”)
  8. Paste your signature in the editor (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V)
  9. Signature defaults:
    • Under “For new emails use”, select Sunflower Signature
    • Under “On reply/forward use”, select Sunflower Signature (or “None”)
  10. Click “Save Changes” at the bottom
💡 Tip: Test your signature by composing a new email to yourself!
